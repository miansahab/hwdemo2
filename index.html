About HWSD

A nurturing education experience with a future-ready focus.

The Harper Woods School District is dedicated to providing a challenging curriculum aligned with every classroom. Through community collaborations, steadfast educators, and innovative programs, HWSD supports a successful and sustainable future for all stakeholders.
