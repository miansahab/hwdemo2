About Beacon

Prepared to learn and succeed each day.

At Beacon Elementary School, the mission is to ensure every child is prepared to learn and succeed each day. The school is committed to high-quality early childhood education with a curriculum designed to address academic, social, and emotional needs through literacy and numeracy development.

Core Subjects

Reading, writing, mathematics, science, physical education, music, art, and computer literacy.

Small Class Sizes

Individualized attention and support for GSRP, kindergarten, first, and second-grade students.

STEAM Learning

Weekly hands-on investigations that foster curiosity and critical thinking.

1-to-1 Technology

Modern instructional technology supports an engaging learning environment.
